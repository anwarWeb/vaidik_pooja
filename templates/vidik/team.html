Our Team


Pt. Shivnarayan Awasthi


Pandit Shivnarayan Awasthi ji is well qualified pandit with his training and education from Maharishi Ved Vigyan Vidyapeeth. He has served at many places and is currently living in Kanpur, India. The priests or priests are called in private and public yajnas and other rituals for the Ved Mantrochar and in return they are given away by giving alms and donations. The purpose of this type of chanting is to live the atmosphere of that work with the spiritual progress of the listener while listening to it with a calm mind. Most of the priests are vegetarian due to spiritual reasons because they are usually expected to keep themselves pure with body and mind
